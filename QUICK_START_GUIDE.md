## Essential First Steps

### 1. Dashboard Familiarization
- View progress indicators for all requirements
- Check current completion status
- Identify priority areas needing attention

### 2. Practice Hours Setup
- **Manual Entry**: Add individual time periods
- **Weekly Calculator**: Automated calculation from contracted hours
- **Target**: 450 hours over 3 years

### 3. CPD Planning
- **Goal**: 35 hours minimum over 3 years
- **Mix**: Participatory (20+ hours) and Non-participatory (max 15 hours)
- **Types**: Conferences, courses, reading, research

### 4. Start Your First Reflection
- Use AI assistance for guidance
- Follow the 6-stage reflection model
- Link to relevant NMC Code sections

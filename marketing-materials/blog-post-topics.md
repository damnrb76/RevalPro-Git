## Content Distribution Strategy

### Publishing Schedule
- **Weekly Blog Posts**: Publish one new article every week, alternating between evergreen and timely content
- **Monthly Feature**: Detailed case study or user story
- **Quarterly Special**: Comprehensive guides coinciding with common revalidation deadlines

### Promotion Channels
- Email newsletter to registered users
- Social media promotion across nursing groups
- Cross-posting on nursing forums and professional networks
- Targeted promotion to NHS Trust education departments

### Content Repurposing
- Convert comprehensive guides into downloadable PDFs
- Create infographics from statistical content
- Develop short video tutorials from how-to content
- Use blog insights for webinar and workshop material

### Engagement Strategy
- Enable comments on all blog posts
- Create dedicated discussion threads for major topics
- Encourage user submissions for case studies
- Regular polls and surveys to identify content gaps

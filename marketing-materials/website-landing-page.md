## FAQ Section

# Frequently Asked Questions

**Is RevalPro officially endorsed by the NMC?**
RevalPro is not affiliated with or endorsed by the NMC. However, our app is designed to comply with all current NMC revalidation requirements and generates documentation in NMC-approved formats.

**Will RevalPro stay updated with NMC requirement changes?**
Yes! We constantly monitor NMC guidelines and update the app promptly whenever requirements change. You'll always be working with the most current standards.

**Is my data secure?**
Absolutely. RevalPro prioritizes your privacy by storing data locally on your device first. Optional cloud backup uses end-to-end encryption, and we adhere to UK GDPR and NHS Digital security standards.

**Can I export my data if I decide to stop using RevalPro?**
Yes. You can export all your revalidation data in standard formats at any time. We believe your professional information belongs to you.

**What if I need help using the app?**
RevalPro includes comprehensive in-app help, video tutorials, and our AI assistant can answer most questions instantly. Premium users also receive priority email support.

**Does RevalPro work offline?**
Yes! Most features work fully offline. Your data synchronizes when you reconnect to the internet.

**Can I use RevalPro on multiple devices?**
Yes. Your account works across smartphones, tablets, and computers, with your data synchronized securely between all your devices.

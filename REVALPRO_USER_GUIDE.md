## Getting Started

### What is RevalPro?

RevalPro is a specialized application designed for UK NHS nurses to manage their NMC revalidation requirements efficiently. The platform helps track:
- **Practice Hours** (minimum 450 hours over 3 years)
- **Continuing Professional Development (CPD)** (minimum 35 hours)
- **Reflective Accounts** (5 written reflections)
- **Feedback** from patients and colleagues
- **Health & Character Declarations**
- **Professional Development Records**

### System Requirements

- **Modern web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Internet connection** for synchronization
- **JavaScript enabled**
- **Cookies enabled** for authentication

### Privacy & Data Security

- **Local Storage First**: Your data is primarily stored on your device for maximum privacy
- **GDPR Compliant**: Full compliance with UK and EU data protection regulations
- **No Unnecessary Uploads**: Sensitive revalidation data stays on your device
- **Secure Authentication**: Industry-standard security for account access

## Core Revalidation Features

### Practice Hours Management

**Purpose**: Track the mandatory 450 practice hours over your 3-year revalidation period.

#### **Manual Entry Method**

1. **Navigate to Practice Hours**
   - Click "Revalidation Elements" → "Practice Hours"

2. **Add New Entry**
   - Click "Add Practice Hours" button
   - Select "Manual Entry" tab

3. **Complete Entry Form**
   - **Start Date**: Beginning of practice period
   - **End Date**: End of practice period
   - **Total Hours**: Hours worked in this period
   - **Work Setting**: Hospital, Community, Care Home, etc.
   - **Scope of Practice**: Adult, Child, Mental Health, etc.
   - **Registration**: Primary or Additional
   - **Notes**: Optional additional details

4. **Save Entry**
   - Review information for accuracy
   - Click "Save Practice Hours"

#### **Weekly Hours Calculator**

**For automated calculation based on contracted hours:**

1. **Select Weekly Calculator Tab**
   - Choose "Weekly Calculator" option
   - More accurate for regular employment

2. **Set Up Configuration**
   - **Start Date**: Beginning of calculation period
   - **End Date**: End of calculation period
   - **Weekly Contracted Hours**: Your standard hours per week

3. **Weekly Breakdown**
   - System calculates week-by-week entries
   - **Mark non-working weeks** (holidays, sick leave)
   - **Adjust individual weeks** as needed
   - **Only completed weeks** count toward totals

4. **Review and Save**
   - Check completed vs pending hours
   - Save configuration for ongoing tracking

#### **Practice Hours Best Practices**

- **Regular Updates**: Enter hours monthly for accuracy
- **Detailed Records**: Include specific work settings
- **Varied Experience**: Demonstrate breadth of practice
- **Supporting Evidence**: Keep timesheets and rotas
- **NMC Compliance**: Ensure hours meet scope requirements

### CPD (Continuing Professional Development)

**Purpose**: Document 35 hours of learning and development activities.

#### **Adding CPD Records**

1. **Access CPD Section**
   - Navigate to "Revalidation Elements" → "CPD Records"

2. **Create New CPD Entry**
   - Click "Add CPD Record"
   - Complete detailed form

3. **CPD Entry Details**
   - **Date**: When learning occurred
   - **Title**: Name of activity/course
   - **Description**: Detailed learning outcomes
   - **Hours**: Duration of activity
   - **Type**: Participatory or Non-participatory
   - **Relevance to NMC Code**: How it relates to professional standards
   - **Evidence**: Upload certificates or documentation

#### **CPD Categories**

**Participatory Learning** (minimum 20 hours):
- Conferences and seminars
- Training courses
- Workshops
- Webinars with interaction
- Team meetings with learning content

**Non-participatory Learning** (maximum 15 hours):
- Reading professional journals
- Online courses without interaction
- Self-directed study
- Research activities
- Policy reviews

#### **CPD Planning Tips**

- **Spread learning** across the 3-year cycle
- **Align with professional goals** and development needs
- **Mix participatory and non-participatory** activities
- **Link to NMC Code** standards for relevance
- **Keep certificates** and supporting evidence

### Feedback Management

**Purpose**: Collect and document feedback from patients, carers, and colleagues.

#### **Feedback Requirements**

- **5 pieces of feedback** minimum over 3 years
- **Mix of sources**: patients, carers, colleagues
- **Written format** with specific examples
- **Anonymous options** available
- **Constructive and developmental** focus

#### **Recording Feedback**

1. **Navigate to Feedback Section**
   - Go to "Revalidation Elements" → "Feedback"

2. **Add Feedback Entry**
   - Click "Add Feedback"
   - Choose feedback type

3. **Feedback Details**
   - **Date Received**: When feedback was given
   - **Source**: Patient, Carer, Colleague, Manager
   - **Method**: Verbal, Written, Survey, Formal review
   - **Content**: Detailed feedback received
   - **Action Taken**: How you responded/improved
   - **Anonymous**: Option to anonymize source

#### **Feedback Collection Strategies**

- **Proactive requests**: Ask patients and colleagues
- **Formal processes**: Use workplace feedback systems
- **Informal conversations**: Document valuable insights
- **360-degree reviews**: Comprehensive colleague feedback
- **Thank you notes**: Patient appreciation letters

### Reflective Accounts

**Purpose**: Write 5 reflective accounts demonstrating learning and professional development.

#### **Reflection Structure**

**NMC-Approved Format:**
1. **Description**: What happened?
2. **Feelings**: How did you feel about it?
3. **Evaluation**: What was good/bad about the experience?
4. **Analysis**: Why did things happen this way?
5. **Conclusion**: What else could you have done?
6. **Action Plan**: How will you improve future practice?

#### **Creating Reflections**

1. **Access Reflection Tool**
   - Navigate to "Revalidation Elements" → "Reflections"

2. **Start New Reflection**
   - Click "Write New Reflection"
   - Use AI-powered guidance

3. **Complete Reflection**
   - **Title**: Descriptive summary
   - **Date**: When incident/learning occurred
   - **NMC Code Links**: Relevant professional standards
   - **Content**: Follow the 6-stage structure
   - **Learning Outcomes**: What you gained
   - **Future Application**: How you'll use learning

#### **Reflection Topics**

**Suitable reflection areas:**
- **Clinical incidents**: Learning from practice situations
- **Professional development**: Course learning application
- **Ethical dilemmas**: Complex decision-making
- **Team working**: Collaboration and communication
- **Patient interactions**: Care delivery experiences
- **Innovation**: New practice implementations

#### **AI-Assisted Reflection**

- **Guided prompts** for each reflection stage
- **Professional language** suggestions
- **NMC Code references** automatically suggested
- **Structure templates** for different scenarios
- **Privacy-first**: All content stays on your device

## Troubleshooting & Support

### Common Issues

#### **Login and Access Problems**

**Issue: Cannot access demo account**
- **Solution**: Clear browser cache and cookies
- **Alternative**: Try different browser or incognito mode
- **Check**: Ensure JavaScript is enabled

**Issue: Forgot password**
- **Solution**: Use "Forgot Password" link on login page
- **Process**: Check email for reset instructions
- **Backup**: Contact support if email not received

**Issue: Account locked or suspended**
- **Solution**: Contact customer support
- **Information**: Provide account email and details
- **Resolution**: Support team will investigate

#### **Data and Sync Issues**

**Issue: Data not saving**
- **Check**: Browser local storage capacity
- **Solution**: Clear unnecessary browser data
- **Backup**: Export data before clearing

**Issue: Missing entries**
- **Verify**: Check date filters and search terms
- **Review**: Confirm save operation completed
- **Restore**: Use backup if available

**Issue: Export not working**
- **Browser**: Try different browser
- **Popup**: Check popup blocker settings
- **Format**: Try alternative export format

#### **Performance Issues**

**Issue: Slow loading**
- **Connection**: Check internet connection
- **Browser**: Clear cache and refresh
- **Device**: Close unnecessary applications

**Issue: Features not working**
- **Browser**: Update to latest version
- **JavaScript**: Ensure JavaScript enabled
- **Compatibility**: Try different browser

REVALPRO - COMPLETE FEATURES, FUNCTIONALITY & PRICING
=====================================================

CORE NMC REVALIDATION REQUIREMENTS
===================================

PRACTICE HOURS TRACKING
------------------------
• Track 450 hours of practice (900 for dual registration)
• Log work setting, scope of practice, dates, and hours
• Multiple entries supported with detailed notes
• Progress tracking against NMC requirements

CPD RECORDS MANAGEMENT
----------------------
• Record 35 hours of Continuing Professional Development
• Minimum 20 hours must be participatory learning
• Track relevance to NMC Code sections
• Support for attachments and detailed descriptions
• Categorize learning types and activities

FEEDBACK COLLECTION
-------------------
• Collect and organize 5 pieces of practice-related feedback
• Sources: patients, colleagues, supervisors, managers
• Include reflection on feedback received
• Attachment support for documentation
• Structured feedback analysis tools

REFLECTIVE ACCOUNTS
-------------------
• Create 5 reflective accounts using various models:
  - Standard reflection
  - Gibbs' Reflective Cycle
  - Johns' Model
  - Driscoll's Model
  - Kolb's Experiential Learning
  - Rolfe's Framework
• Link reflections to specific NMC Code sections
• Unlimited storage for reflective writing

REFLECTIVE DISCUSSION
---------------------
• Record details of discussions with other NMC registrants
• Track participant details and discussion outcomes
• Document learning from peer interactions

HEALTH & CHARACTER DECLARATIONS
-------------------------------
• Complete self-declarations for good health and character
• Annual confirmation requirements
• Track declaration history and updates

CONFIRMATION DETAILS
--------------------
• Record confirmer information and submission tracking
• Manage confirmer contact details
• Track confirmation status and dates

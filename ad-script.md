## Production Notes:

1. **Voice Style:** Professional yet warm British accent (ideally female to match nursing demographics)
2. **Pacing:** Moderate, allowing viewers to absorb the information about features
3. **Music:** Uplifting, modern but not overpowering - should convey progress and relief
4. **Visuals:** Clean transitions between app screens, with occasional shots of nurses using the app in hospital settings
5. **Color Palette:** Use RevalPro's branded colors throughout (blues, teals, purples)
6. **Text Overlays:** Keep minimal, focusing on key statistics and feature names
7. **Length:** Aim for 90-120 seconds total
